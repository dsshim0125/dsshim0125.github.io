Dongseok Shim

I am a Research Scientist at Creative AI Lab, Sony Group Corporation in Tokyo, Japan. I received my Ph.D. from the Lab for Autonomous Robotics Research (LARR) at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. H. Jin Kim. Previously, I was a Research Intern with the Seed Vision at ByteDance USA.

My research focuses on multimodal generative AI and robotics, including video-to-audio generation, text-to-3D generation, multimodal human motion generation, depth estimation, and reinforcement learning.
